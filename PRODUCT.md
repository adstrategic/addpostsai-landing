# Product

## Register
brand

## Users
Content creators, startup founders, and social media managers looking to save time cross-posting.

## Product Purpose
A landing page to drive waitlist signups and free trials for ADDPOSTS, a simple and affordable social media cross-posting tool.

## Brand Personality
Modern, simple, bold, and vibrant. Dark mode with claymorphism depth.

## Anti-references
Complex enterprise SaaS, flat gray dashboards, boring or overly loud interfaces.

## Design Principles
- Practice what you preach
- Show, don't tell
- Expert confidence
- Vibrant depth (Claymorphism)

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA contrast for text, smooth reduced-motion alternatives.
